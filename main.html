Discover the insight in YouTube Video Title

Introduction

When you serach the video on YouTube, some titles are attractive so you will click the video and watch it. Typically the video is classified by the content of video. In this project, we will work on the video title and discover the community in videos. And we hope to discover new insights based on the analysis of video titles.

Workflow

Our Project consists of four parts.
  • Get & Clean Dataset
  • Encode Video Title
  • Build Graph
  • Community Detection
Get & Clean Dataset
  • Dataset: YouTube Dataset on Kaggle
  • Website: https://www.kaggle.com/datasnaek/youtube-new?tdsourcetag=s_pctim_aiomsg
  • We choose the titles in YouTube US
  • Remove emoji and Korean words + sampling
  • 238 titles and 5 categories
Encode Video Title
Pretrained deep learning is used to encode the video title. The state of art NLP model BERT can help us convert the sentence to vector.
Build Graph
The graph we build is based on the dissimilarity of titles. We calcualte the pairwise distance by cosine distance. Besides, we use PCA to do dimension reduction (from 768 dimensions to 48 dimensions).
Community Detection
We have tried three algorithm to detect the communities. We found that the Girvan-Newman algorithm give the best result.
